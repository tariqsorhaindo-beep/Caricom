About CariCom

Authentic Caribbean flavors, crafted with care.

Our Story

CariCom, short for Caribbean Condiments, was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to bring true, authentic Caribbean flavour to the modern world. Born from a love of proper homemade sauces, seasonings, and the kind of food that brings people together, CariCom was created to celebrate the bold, vibrant taste of the Caribbean in a way that feels both rooted in tradition and ready for today's kitchens.

At the heart of the brand is a belief that great flavour does not need to be artificial or overcomplicated. That is why CariCom is built around 100% natural ingredients, with no artificial flavouring, no artificial colouring, and no unnecessary additives. Our focus is on honest ingredients, real flavour, and products that deliver the warmth, heat, freshness, and depth that Caribbean food is known for.

From fiery pepper sauces to fresh green seasonings, every CariCom product is made to bring character to everyday meals. Whether it is drizzled over chicken, stirred into rice, used as a marinade, or added to your favourite dish for an instant lift, CariCom is about making proper Caribbean flavour easy to enjoy.

We are proud of our Caribbean identity and the richness that comes with it. CariCom is more than just a condiment brand — it is a celebration of culture, taste, and authenticity. Our goal is to create products that feel familiar to those who grew up with these flavours, while introducing a wider audience to Caribbean condiments done properly.

As we continue to grow, our commitment stays the same: authentic Caribbean flavour, modern presentation, and natural ingredients you can trust. CariCom brings the taste of the Caribbean to the table — bold, honest, and made properly.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring authentic Caribbean flavour to the modern world through bold, natural condiments made with real ingredients, honest recipes, and a premium standard people can trust.

Our Values

  • Authenticity: We stay true to real Caribbean flavour, culture, and food traditions.
  • Quality: We care about taste, ingredients, consistency, and presentation.
  • Community: Food brings people together, and CariCom should feel rooted in warmth, sharing, and connection.
  • Trust: Customers should feel confident in what they're buying, from ingredients to product quality to brand standards.
  • Flavour First: Everything starts with proper taste. The product has to deliver before anything else.
  • Craft: CariCom should feel made with care, intention, and attention to detail.
