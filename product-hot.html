Proper Pepper Sauce – Hot Ting

Hot Ting is our bold Caribbean pepper sauce made for people who want real fire without losing flavour. Bright, punchy, and full of character, it cuts through rich food and lifts everyday meals instantly.

Size: 148ml glass bottle

How to Use

Hot Ting is your go-to Caribbean pepper sauce for adding proper heat and bold flavour to everyday food. Drizzle it over chicken, fish, rice, beef, patties, fries and wraps, or stir it into marinades, dips and sauces when you want an extra kick.

Ingredients

  • Scotch bonnet peppers
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Vinegar
  • Salt
  • Thyme
  • Spring onion
  • Lime juice or lemon juice
  • Mustard
  • Sugar
  • Water
