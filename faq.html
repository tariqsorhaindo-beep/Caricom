Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about CariCom.

What is CariCom?

CariCom is a Caribbean condiment brand bringing authentic island flavour to everyday food through bold pepper sauces and seasonings made with real ingredients and proper character.

How spicy are your products?

Our range is designed to suit different taste preferences. Hot Ting brings a proper kick, Mild Ting offers a gentler level of heat, and Green Ting is more focused on fresh, herby flavour and everyday seasoning.

How should I use the products?

Our products are made to be versatile. Use Hot Ting and Mild Ting as table sauces, dipping additions, or flavour boosters for chicken, rice, eggs, wraps and more. Green Ting works especially well as a marinade, cooking base, or seasoning for meat, fish, veg and rice dishes.

Are your products made with natural ingredients?

Yes — CariCom is built around real, flavourful ingredients with no artificial flavouring or colouring. Final ingredient details will always be shown clearly on each product label and product page.

Are your products suitable for gifting?

Yes. CariCom products make great gifts for food lovers, spice lovers, and anyone who enjoys bold Caribbean flavour. Our bundles are especially good for gifting and trying the full range.

Where can I buy CariCom products?

CariCom products will be available directly through our website, with future opportunities for stockists and wholesale partners as the brand grows.
