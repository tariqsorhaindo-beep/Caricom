Proper Pepper Seasoning – Green Ting

Green Ting is a vibrant green seasoning packed with fresh flavour and everyday versatility. Use it as a marinade, cooking base, dipping spoonful, or flavour booster for meats, fish, veg, and rice dishes.

Size: 150ml glass jar

How to Use

Green Ting is best used as a fresh Caribbean seasoning — marinate chicken, fish, lamb, prawns or veg, or stir it into rice, stews and sauces for bold, herby everyday flavour.

Ingredients

  • Spring onion
  • Scotch bonnet peppers
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Thyme
  • Parsley
  • Bell pepper
  • Ginger
  • Salt
  • Vinegar
  • Lime juice
  • Oil
  • Water
