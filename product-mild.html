Proper Pepper Sauce – Mild Ting

Mild Ting brings the same Caribbean character in a gentler format. It's bright, savoury, versatile, and easy to reach for every day — perfect for people who love flavour-first condiments with a softer kick.

Size: 148ml glass bottle

How to Use

Mild Ting is made for everyday flavour. Drizzle it over chicken, rice, wraps, burgers, eggs and grilled veg, or use it in dips and marinades when you want Caribbean character without too much heat.

Ingredients

  • Scotch bonnet peppers
  • Bell pepper
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Vinegar
  • Salt
  • Spring onion
  • Thyme
  • Lime juice or lemon juice
  • Sugar
  • Water
